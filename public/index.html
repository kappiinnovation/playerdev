About Us

Complete Player Development Program with Jawann Oldham

We are a Player Developmental Program

We want Student Athletes to join our basketball and Strength & Conditioning program. We want Student Athletes that have academic goals through there athletic hearts. We at Player Developmental Program want to take you to your next level of life in becoming a student athlete.

To train you to improve you to transform you to support you to energize you into your Transcendence in your mind and body. For those of you who want to go beyond we want you to join our U.S.C.P. University Scholarship College Program. This program is for those who are Committed driven inspired focused dedicated and have there heart in there academics and Athletic abilities that Player Developmental Program will inspire to option that athletic scholarship to a Division 2 or Division 3 University or College.

Even if you not ready to go that far we still want to help you achieve your basketball basic medium or advanced skill level training. We want to set you apart from the rest so you can be your best physically and mentally . We want you to have that basketball court presences to be a contributor and a valued member of your team.

We want you to produce on the court and always pull your weight along side your teammates. Player Developmental Program has the knowledge history experience drive technical expertise and years of experience to start you on your journey.

"It’s all about the journey there will be many stops along the way but your life’s journey will be the most imprint on your lifelong travels into your future." - Start your Journey with Player Developmental Program Academy. Core Values of Director and Head Coach. Jawann Oldham .N.B.A. Retired. National Basketball Retired Players Association

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the difference between Player development and Acadamy?

    Player Development and Academy are linked together through the evolution of Development. It is beneficial for the students to be mentally and physically educated within a Player Developmental program that has an Academy influence.

  • Who can join the program?

    Male and females can join the Basketball Developmental Training program.- the ages must be 12 to 17 years of age and must be of sound mind and body.

  • When will the program be held?

    Mid Summer’s Afternoon Basketball Camp Program- Monday-Tuesday- Wednesday- Thursday. Starting July Monday 11-2022 - 11:00 am to 3:00 pm. Ending August 13th 2022- young Men and young Women-Ages 11 to 17 years old
    Fall and Winter Developmental Training Program/ and U.S.C.P. / University Scholarship College Program. Please inquire about this advanced program with Head Coach and Director Jawann Oldham.

  • Will Jawann Oldham personally take the class?

    Yes, Jawann Oldham will be doing all of the Coaching during practices. May have one assist Coach.
